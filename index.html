What Is Commercial Auto Insurance?

Commercial auto insurance is a type of coverage designed for businesses that use vehicles for their operations. Unlike personal auto insurance, commercial auto insurance offers additional protections suited for business-related risks, such as transporting goods, clients, or employees. This type of insurance is essential for any business that relies on a vehicle or fleet of vehicles to operate daily.
